An android app to manage and control netis routers. Has been downloaded 40k+ times from gooogle play, making it the most popular product I've developed personally so far.

[Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=dev.sakib.mynetis)

# Features


- See connected devices.
- Block devices with just one tap.
- Set speed limit on devices.
- Block websites and apps in your network.
- Change network name and password.
